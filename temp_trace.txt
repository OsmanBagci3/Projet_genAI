Query: What are the 5 most recent appointments with patient names?
Provider: claude
Route: SQL_QUERY
SQL Valid: True
Num Rows: 5
Duration: 14.55s

Generated SQL:
SELECT 
    a.appointment_id,
    a.appointment_date,
    p.first_name,
    p.last_name
FROM appointments a
JOIN patients p ON a.patient_id = p.patient_id
ORDER BY a.appointment_date DESC
LIMIT 5;
